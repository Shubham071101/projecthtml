  • Welcome

    Bombay Engineering University, an English medium co-educational college was established in the year 1977 by Priyansh Rawal, with a view to cater the educational needs of students in the locality as their were no college in this locality and students have to commute for hours.

    The college began functioning in small rented premise. Initially the college began with two departments Civil Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. There where few teacher who gave their best in teaching the students in shifts. The principle always says that the college has always inspired their students to think out of the box and Today their are many alumini who are ex-students of the college.

    The university don't discriminate among students and give their best to encorage them to participate in different activites like dance, vocal, art etc. The college has provided all the facilities needed for students like clean toilets, Drinking facilites, etc.
